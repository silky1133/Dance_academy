Class Information

Ballet

Our ballet classes are designed for dancers of all ages and levels. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced ballerina, our expert instructors will guide you through classical ballet techniques, focusing on posture, strength, flexibility, and grace.

Class Schedule: Mondays & Wednesdays, 5:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Jazz

Join our jazz classes to explore the vibrant and energetic world of jazz dance. Learn a variety of jazz styles, including Broadway, lyrical, and contemporary jazz, while improving your technique, musicality, and performance skills.

Class Schedule: Tuesdays & Thursdays, 6:30 PM - 7:30 PM

Tap

Tap into the rhythm and excitement of tap dance! Our tap classes focus on developing intricate footwork, rhythm, and musicality, while exploring a range of tap styles from classic to modern. No experience necessary!

Class Schedule: Fridays, 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Hip-hop

Get ready to groove and move in our hip-hop classes! Learn the latest hip-hop moves and choreography while building strength, coordination, and confidence. Our dynamic instructors will keep you energized and inspired!

Class Schedule: Saturdays, 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Contemporary

Experience the freedom and expression of contemporary dance! Our contemporary classes blend elements of ballet, jazz, and modern dance, allowing dancers to explore their emotions and creativity through movement.

Class Schedule: Sundays, 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM
